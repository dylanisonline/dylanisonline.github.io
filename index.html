👋 Hey there!

Purpose

This website documents my hands-on IT lab environment, built to simulate the infrastructure of a small enterprise network. Each project is interconnected as part of one larger environment, including Active Directory, domain-joined workstations, Group Policy, file services, network segmentation, permissions management, and future infrastructure services.

The goal of this lab is to move beyond isolated tutorials and build a realistic environment where each technology supports the others. Through this project, I am developing practical experience in system administration, networking, identity management, troubleshooting, and technical documentation.

Another Note!

As a lifelong gamer, I wanted this lab to feel like more than just a collection of servers, users, and network diagrams. The naming conventions throughout the environment are inspired by the worlds, characters, and themes found in Hideo Kojima’s games.

This gives the lab a bit of personality while still keeping the structure realistic and professional. Servers, workstations, users, groups, and other resources follow a consistent naming approach, but with references that make the environment more memorable and enjoyable to build.

Think of it as a small enterprise network with a little tactical espionage flair.
